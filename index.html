Working Towards a Sustainable Future



As the human population grows to reach 8 billion, more non-renewable resources are being exploited and an increased strain is being placed on the environment. For instance, the mining itself of rare metals or the extraction of crude oil have a catastrophic impact on the environment as they destroy habitats, contaminate nearby streams, and make the exploited area unhabitable. As the demand for resources like these arises, we need to find new, more effective ways to protect and preserve the environment.

On this website, we will explore and examine some of the existing solutions that exemplify sustainable feedback loops.
